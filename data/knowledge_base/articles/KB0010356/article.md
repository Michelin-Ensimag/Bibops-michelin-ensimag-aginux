**Summary**

This article explains what Michelin’s limits are for both their O365 Web and 2016 Outlook client. If you go over these limits, then you will lose some functionality, such as sending emails.

**Symptoms**

- What is my mailbox size
- Unable to send attachment in email
- Unable to send or receive email (mailbox full)

**Details**

| *Steps*<br> | *Actions*<br> | *Illustrations*<br> |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **1**<br> | At May 2017, mailbox quotas defined by Michelin for Office 365 are based on your license type:<br> | - **License F1 = 2 Gb**
- **License E1 = 50 Gb**
- **License E3 = 100 Gb** |
| **2**<br> | Max file attachment size for**All Licenses**:<br> | **10 Mb**<br> |
| **3**<br> | Shared mailbox and functional mailbox size are limited at:<br> | **50 Gb**<br> |

**Limit cannot be modified, you can do a clean-up process**, refer to:[KB0010576](https://michelin.service-now.com/sp?id=kb_article&sysparm_article=KB0010576).
