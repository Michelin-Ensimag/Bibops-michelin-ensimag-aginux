**Summary:**

The loss of information security is caused by deliberately registering a public domain name very close to a Michelin’s one. Usually cybersquatting domains also use a very close graphical charter Micheiin.com, mlchelin.com, michellin.com

**Definition:**

A cybersquatting consists to register a public domain name close to a Michelin's one (example: mlchelin.com, michellin.com …). A lambda user will not see immediately the difference. It's possible that the website on the domain rip off the user or gives wrong information leading to bad company image.

**Solution:**

Within the incident details, capture the following information for**all**assets:

- **Date and time of detection**
- **Exact domain name:**Eg. michellin.com
- **Where user saw this domain:**Eg. Google / Email / Ad …
- **User has visited the website:**Yes/No
- **If YES, what is the type of content on the website:**Redirection on competitor / item selling / pornography

**Details:**

Once the incident details are obtained, perform following actions:

- **Assign the incident to WW-XX-CSIRT**
- Inform region if it's possible

**Symptoms:**

Fake website with domain name very close to a Michelin one
