**Summary**

This article explains what to do if you are not able to delete a calendar in Outlook O365.

**Solution**

Change the name of the calendar, restart the client and try to delete it again.

**Details**

**![image](Pasted%20image_1.png)**Primary Calendar cannot be deleted.

| *Steps*<br> | *Actions*<br> |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **1**<br> | If you have another calendar open, you can instead close it or hide your main calendar.<br> |
| **2**<br> | On the Outlook Mail Navigation bar click the app launcher and then click 'Calendar'<br> |
| **3**<br> | Under the Outlook Calendar navigation bar, choose Calendar.<br> |
| **4**<br> | Select the calendar you want to remove and click on it, then “Edit”, change the name of the calendar.<br> |
| **5**<br> | Exit the application or browser were Outlook is open and then access it again.<br> |
| **6**<br> | Now you should be able to remove the calendar from “My Calendars”<br> |
