**Do not always assume that if the email looks as if it came from a company email that it is genuine, it is VERY easy to spoof email addresses.**

**Summary**

A Social Engineering / Phishing attack is when you receive a suspicious email or suspicious phone call. It could be asking to provide confidential information. It can although contain link which lead to malicious or fake website.

**Symptoms**

If you received an email or phone call and you believe is suspicious, please call the Service desk. It could be asking to you to provide confidential information or possibly take you to a link which could be malicious.

**Solution**

Call the[Service Desk](https://michelingroup.sharepoint.com/sites/ServiceCatalog/SitePages/GetSupport.aspx).
